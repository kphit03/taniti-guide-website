Getting to Taniti

Almost all visitors arrive in Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that accommodates small jets and propeller planes. Expansion plans are underway to enable larger jets to land on the island in the coming years.

Ground Transportation

Visitors can explore Taniti through various ground transportation options:

  • Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.
  • Private buses connect other parts of the island.
  • Taxis are available in Taniti City.
  • Rental cars are available near the airport.
  • Bikes and helmets can be rented from several vendors (helmets are required by law).

Taniti City is fairly flat, making it highly walkable. Many tourists stay in the Merriton Landing area, which is easy to explore on foot.
