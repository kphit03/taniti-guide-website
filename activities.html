Entertainment in Taniti

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano. However, there are other activities, including:

  • Visiting a local history museum
  • Chartered fishing tours
  • Snorkeling
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest
  • Visiting pubs, including a microbrewery
  • Dancing at a new dance club
  • Seeing a movie
  • Taking helicopter rides
  • Playing at an arcade
  • Visiting art galleries
  • Bowling
  • Golfing at a nine-hole course (opening next year)

Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
