Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of power outlets are used in Taniti?

Taniti uses 120-volt outlets, the same as in the United States.

Are there restrictions on alcohol sales?

Alcohol cannot be sold or served between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age is 18, but enforcement is not strict.

Is English widely spoken in Taniti?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. However, in rural areas, especially among older residents, English is rarely spoken.

What healthcare facilities are available?

Taniti has one hospital with multilingual staff and several clinics available for medical assistance.

Is Taniti safe for tourists?

Violent crime is very rare, but there are increasing reports of pickpocketing and petty theft. Exercise caution in crowded areas.

Are businesses open on national holidays?

Many tourist attractions and restaurants are closed on national holidays. Visitors should plan their schedules accordingly.

What currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar, but many businesses also accept euros and yen. Banks provide currency exchange, and most businesses accept major credit cards.

What are some unique attractions in Taniti?

Popular attractions include Yellow Leaf Bay, the rainforest, Taniti’s active volcano, and Merriton Landing’s cultural spots.

When is the best time to visit Taniti?

The dry season (May to October) is the most popular time to visit. Expect fewer crowds during the wet season (November to April).

Are there any tips for first-time visitors?

Bring comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, and insect repellent. Be aware of pickpocketing in crowded areas, and enjoy the local food and culture!

What if I still have questions?

If you have additional questions, feel free to contact us. Our team will be happy to assist you!
